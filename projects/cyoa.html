Cyoa Traveling to Places Project

Context

For my SEP choose your own adventure project, I needed to collaborate with my partner Xin Yang and use our knowledge of github and linking files. My partner and I descided to make a game where you choose where to go.

Content

To start our project we needed to make a plan. We had to start by something that meet the requirements and after we could add more to it. The requirements were:

* google drawing plan * README with the purpose of the project, link to begin, link to the plan and credits that link to our github profiles * at least 10 files * at least one sub-directory

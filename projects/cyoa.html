Cyoa Traveling to Places Project

Context

For my SEP choose your own adventure project, I needed to collaborate with my partner Xin Yang and use our knowledge of github and linking files. My partner and I descided to make a game where you choose where to go.

Content

To start our project we needed to make a plan. We had to start by something that meet the requirements and after we could add more to it. The requirements were:

  • google drawing plan
  • README with the purpose of the project, link to begin, link to the plan and credits that link to our github profiles
  • at least 10 files
  • at least one sub-directory

Reflection

Challenges

A challenge me and my partner had during this project was not being in the same class and having to communicate with each other about git pushing and pulling. However, me and my partner communicated through slack and were able to work very good together.

Takeaways

From this project I learned that communication is key when working with a partner. And that its very important having a plan before beginning because it makes it easier to build your project.

Next Step
